What This Service Is

A practical, business-friendly
security review

We evaluate your environment for common gaps like weak access controls, risky configurations, missing policies, and exposed data. You will leave with clarity, not a scary report full of buzzwords.


How it works

We start with a short intake to understand your systems and goals. Then we review security fundamentals across accounts, devices, cloud tools, email, website, and policies. Finally, we walk you through findings and recommend next steps in a way your whole team can understand.

Best For

  • Businesses without dedicated security staff
  • Teams onboarding new tools (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, cloud apps)
  • Companies that have grown fast and want to tighten processes
