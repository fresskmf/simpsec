Finding the right level for your situation

Stabilize

Quick clarity + guided cleanup

  • ✔ You’re not sure if it’s an incident, but something feels off
  • ✔ You need a fast “what happened / what to do next” plan
  • ✔ You want help locking things down so it doesn’t get worse

Recover

Containment + hands-on investigation

  • ✔ Accounts may be compromised (email, Microsoft/Google, admin logins)
  • ✔ You want us to actively trace what was accessed and shut it down
  • ✔ You need clean next steps: resets, policies, and safer settings

Harden

Business-impact response + recovery support

  • ✔ Downtime, fraud, or data exposure would seriously hurt the business
  • ✔ You want incident handling end-to-end (not just advice)
  • ✔ You need recovery help: verification, restore guidance, and hardening after

Custom Response

Complex incidents + leadership-ready documentation

  • ✔ Multiple systems/vendors are involved (SaaS, endpoints, servers, remote teams)
  • ✔ You need tighter coordination, escalation paths, and faster response
  • ✔ You want clean reporting for leadership, insurance, or compliance
