What This Service Is

We evaluate your web apps, APIs, integrations, and AI tools for common security mistakes and risky behavior. You get a prioritized list of issues, proof of impact, and clear fixes your team can actually implement.

We scope what to test, review how the app works, then run a mix of automated checks and targeted manual testing. Findings come with clear severity, impact, and fix guidance. If you want, we retest after changes to confirm the risk is closed.
