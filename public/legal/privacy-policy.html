Quick summary

This Privacy Policy explains how SimpSec, LLC (“SimpSec,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or contact us. It applies only to website interactions and does not override privacy terms in any separate client agreements.

Overview

SimpSec operates this website to provide information about our cybersecurity, IT, automation, and consulting services. We are committed to handling information responsibly and in line with security best practices.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide – such as name, email address, company name, phone number, and message content when you submit a form or contact us.
  • Technical information – such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.
  • Usage data – aggregated analytics data about how visitors use the Site.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and communicate with you
  • Provide and improve the Site
  • Evaluate interest in our services
  • Maintain security, logging, and abuse prevention
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only in limited circumstances, including:

  • With service providers who help operate the Site (hosting, analytics, email)
  • To comply with law, legal process, or government requests
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of SimpSec or others

Cookies & Tracking

Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to operate the Site, analyze traffic, and improve performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Security

Security practices

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal information. To make a request, contact us using the information below.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and the “Effective” date will be revised. Continued use of the Site after changes means you accept the updated Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us through our contact page. Please include “Privacy Policy” in the subject for faster routing.
