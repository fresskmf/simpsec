Words from the founder

I’m Vanessa and I'm very passionate about helping creators, small businesses, and non-tech folks feel confident and secure online.

With my background in IT support and network administration, I saw firsthand how overwhelming technology and cybersecurity can feel when all you really want is to run your business. Too often, people are left confused, intimidated, or unsure where to start and I knew there had to be a better way. That’s why I want to make security simple.

As a mom, and as someone who started a business from scratch, I know how important it is to feel supported in today’s digital world. You shouldn’t have to figure it out alone. We’ve got you.
